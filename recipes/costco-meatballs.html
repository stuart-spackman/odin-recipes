Costco Meatballs

Description

I discovered these meatballs less than a year ago and I've gone through periods of really liking them. I bake them in the oven and then cook them up in a sauce pan with some marinara sauce (also purchased from Costco).

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • water
  • seasoning
  • salt
  • durum flour
  • et al

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Place the number of frozen meatballs you want to use on a baking sheet with plenty of space between and in one layer.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and use as desired.

Costco Potstickers

Description

I like these postickers so much that there's been several occasions where I ate the entire bag in one sitting. I used to have a Saturday ritual where I would do an hour long gi jiu jitsu class, an hour long boxing class, and an hour long no gi jiu jitsu class. Then I would come home and eat an entire bag of these potstickers. Maybe someday I'll be able to do that again, but the past couple times I attempted it I failed. Look up the weight of the bag these potstickers come in at your own risk!

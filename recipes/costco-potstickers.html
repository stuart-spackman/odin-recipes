Steps

Pan Fry

  1. Add 1 Tbsp. oil to a nonstick pan and coat evenly.
  2. Place frozen potstickers flat, bottom-side down, leaving space between each.
  3. Add water (see table below) and turn on Medium heat.
  4. Bring to boil. Cover and simmer on Medium heat for 8 minutes.
  5. Uncover. Do not move potstickers. Continue cooking until water evaporates and bottoms of potstickers are lightly browned and... Enjoy a traditional potsticker experience.

Boil

  1. Fill pot with water (or bouillon for more flavor).
  2. Bring to boil. Add frozen potstickers.
  3. Return to boil. Boil 5 to 6 minutes.
  4. Drain well and enjoy!
